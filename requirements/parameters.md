# Evaluation Model Hierarchy: Parameters and Weightings

This document outlines the Weighted Hierarchical Scoring Model (Section 2 of the platform requirements). Scores are entered at the Construct (Level 2) level, and the Parameter (Level 1) score is automatically calculated using the defined weights.

## 1. Technical Skills (Level 1 Parameter)

This is a calculated score based on the following inputs:

| Construct (Level 2 Input) | Weighting | Role/Focus |
|---------------------------|-----------|------------|
| Ball Reception | 25% | Receiving the ball, first-touch, first-touch carry. |
| Ball Distribution / Ball Release | 25% | Short/long passing, static/dynamic release, forehand/backhand, aerials. |
| Ball Carrying / Dribbling | 25% | Ball protection, beating defenders, carrying with vision. |
| Ball Winning / Gaining Possession | 25% | Engaging and winning 1v1 contests, defensive positioning. |

## 2. Athletic Abilities / Physical Attributes (Level 1 Parameter)

This parameter includes a mix of subjective ratings and objective data inputs (see Section 2.3 in Requirements).

| Construct (Level 2 Input) | Weighting | Role/Focus |
|---------------------------|-----------|------------|
| Physical Foundation | 11.1% | Body composition, injury susceptibility, running technique. |
| Athletic Indicators in Match Play | 33.3% | Speed, agility, acceleration, and strength observed during games/drills. |
| Athletic Indicators in Strength & Conditioning | 22.2% | Observed performance in S&C sessions (sprint technique, strength capacity). |
| Measured Athletic Data | 33.3% | (Objective Data Input) Measured metrics like 30m sprint time, T-sprint agility, Lactate testing results. |

## 3. Game Intelligence (Level 1 Parameter)

| Construct (Level 2 Input) | Weighting | Role/Focus |
|---------------------------|-----------|------------|
| Decision-Making | 24% | Choice of skill and efficiency/outcome of the decision. |
| Game Management | 16% | Situation assessment and risk appetite. |
| Availability for the Ball | 20% | Off-ball movement, positioning, and pre-scanning awareness. |
| Defensive Game Understanding | 20% | Intercepting, defensive positioning, and playing the contest effectively. |
| Other / Additional Indicators | 20% | Ingenuity, perception, and speed of processing visual information. |

## 4. Social Competence (Level 1 Parameter)

| Construct (Level 2 Input) | Weighting | Role/Focus |
|---------------------------|-----------|------------|
| Commitment | 20% | Effort, attendance, and engagement in training and team activities. |
| Behaviour | 20% | Influence on the team, interaction with teammates, and attitude. |
| Response to Success and Setbacks | 20% | Player's reaction and management of high/low moments. |
| Role Understanding | 20% | Clarity and acceptance of their specific role within the team structure. |
| Communication | 20% | Quality and effectiveness of on-field and off-field communication. |

## 5. Aptitude / Learn Ability (Level 1 Parameter)

| Construct (Level 2 Input) | Weighting | Role/Focus |
|---------------------------|-----------|------------|
| Development | 30% | Overall progression in athletic, technical, and tactical understanding. |
| Adaptability; Speed of Adjustment | 25% | Quickness in adapting to new tactics, positions, or game scenarios. |
| Input Absorption | 20% | Ability to translate coaching feedback into immediate on-field action. |
| Determination | 25% | Drive to push through challenges, assertiveness, and self-correction. |

## 6. X-Factor (Level 1 Parameter)

| Construct (Level 2 Input) | Weighting | Role/Focus |
|---------------------------|-----------|------------|
| Penalty Corner Specialty | 20% | Offensive and defensive quality during penalty corners. |
| Penalty Shootout & Penalty Stroke | 10% | Performance during decisive penalty situations. |
| Creativity & Smartness | 10% | Unconventional playmaking and tactical cleverness. |
| Essential Personality Traits | 40% | Winner's mentality, handling pressure, and competitive toughness. |
| Personality Traits 'Nice-to-have' | 10% | Strong personality, leadership qualities, and taking responsibility. |
| Presence | 10% | On-field aura and visible impact a player has on others. |
